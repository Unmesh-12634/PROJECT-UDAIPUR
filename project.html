    Explore Your Udaipur

    More Than a Destination. An Experience.

    Ambrai Ghat

    Experience the most picturesque sunset spot in Udaipur with views of the City Palace and Lake Pichola.

    Bahubali Hills

    Trek to this hidden gem for panoramic views of the city and surrounding Aravalli mountains.

    Neemach Mata Temple

    Visit this ancient temple perched on a hilltop offering breathtaking views of Fateh Sagar Lake.

    City Palace

    Discover the splendor of Rajasthan by visiting the majestic City Palace in Udaipur, a regal masterpiece.

    Lake Pichola

    Experience the breathtaking beauty of Lake Pichola in Udaipur, where serene waters create unforgettable moments.

    Jag Mandir

    Discover the enchanting charm of Jag Mandir, a stunning island palace that offers a tranquil escape.

    Sajjangarh Palace

    Discover the breathtaking beauty of Sajjangarh Palace, especially during the monsoon season.

    Vintage Car Museum

    Explore an impressive collection of vintage and classic automobiles in a unique and fascinating setting.

    Jagdish Mandir

    Experience breathtaking architecture, intricate carvings, and spiritual serenity that showcase Rajasthan’s rich heritage.

    Udaipur Zoo

    Visit Gulab Bagh to enjoy its lush gardens, historic charm, and serene ambiance, making it a perfect spot for relaxation.
